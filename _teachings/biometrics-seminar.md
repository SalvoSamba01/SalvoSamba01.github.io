## Overview

This seminar is part of the Biometrics course in the Bachelor's degree program in Computer Science at the University of Catania. It provides an in-depth exploration of biometric systems, with particular focus on fingerprint acquisition and processing.

## Topics Covered

- **Introduction to Biometric Systems**: Overview of biometric identification and authentication
- **Fingerprint Fundamentals**: Ridge patterns, minutiae points, and classification systems
- **Acquisition Techniques**: Optical, ultrasonic, and capacitive fingerprint sensing methods
- **Fingerprint Processing**: Enhancement, feature extraction, and quality assessment
- **Applications**: Criminal justice, civil identification, access control, and mobile authentication
- **Security and Privacy**: Biometric data protection and regulatory considerations
